Pricing Plans

Starter — $29/mo

Recommended for 1–10 crew members

  • Track jobs and customer details
  • Job calendar with to-do checklists
  • Upload before & after job photos
  • Mobile-friendly and easy to use
  • Basic job reports (e.g. total jobs this month, open vs completed)

Team — $59/mo

Recommended for 10–20 crew members

  • Everything in Starter
  • Add users with different access levels
  • Update job status from start to finish
  • Add internal notes & comments
  • Track new leads and customers
  • Upload project files (like permits & plans)
  • Connect with Stripe and QuickBooks

Elite — $119/mo

Recommended for 20+ crew members or multiple teams

  • Everything in Team
  • Manage crews by location or division
  • Automated reminders and task tracking
  • Reports on job speed & crew performance
  • Sync jobs and payments with QuickBooks/Xero
  • Sync leads with HubSpot or Salesforce
  • Priority support & setup assistance
